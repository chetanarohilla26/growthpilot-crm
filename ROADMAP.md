# 🛣️ GrowthPilot CRM Roadmap

> Building a modern AI-powered Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Revenue Operations (RevOps) system for startups and growing businesses.

---

# 🎯 Vision

GrowthPilot CRM aims to demonstrate how modern organizations can build scalable customer relationship management systems using CRM platforms, structured documentation, workflow automation, analytics, and AI-assisted operations.

The long-term goal is to create an open portfolio showcasing CRM best practices, business process documentation, and practical implementations that can be adapted by startups, agencies, and product companies.
